Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with KinSentry. If you're not happy with your subscription, we offer a straightforward refund process.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're unsatisfied with your paid subscription (Guardian or Family), you may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase — no questions asked.

After 30 Days

After the initial 30-day period, refunds are not available for the current billing cycle. However, you can cancel your subscription at any time, and your paid features will remain active until the end of your billing period.

Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscriptions, the 30-day money-back guarantee applies from the date of purchase. After 30 days, you may cancel to prevent renewal, but a prorated refund for the remaining months is not available.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email billing@kinsentry.com with your account email address and reason for the refund. We will process your request within 5-7 business days.

Free Trial

The 30-day Guardian free trial requires no credit card and incurs no charges. If you do not upgrade to a paid plan after the trial, your account simply reverts to the Free tier — no action needed, no charges made.

Contact

For billing questions, email billing@kinsentry.com.
